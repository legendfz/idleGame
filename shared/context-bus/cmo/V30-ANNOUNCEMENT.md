### 1. Reddit r/incremental_games

**标题**: `Journey to the West: Idle v3.0 — Weapon forging, gathering nodes, dungeon bosses with 7 combat mechanics`

**正文**:

v3.0 "Forge Era" is live! This update adds crafting and makes bosses actually dangerous.

🔗 **Play:** https://legendfz.github.io/idleGame/

🔨 **Forging System** — 8 recipes, from ⬜ Common iron swords to 🔴 Hongmeng staves. Collect materials, pick a recipe, craft. Different combos = different stats. Success rates range from 95% to 15%. Failure returns partial materials.

```
🔥 Hongmeng Staff recipe:
  鸿蒙之核 ×3 + 混沌精华 ×10 + 圣物 ×5
  → 🔴 Hongmeng Staff | Success: 15%
```

⛏️ **Gathering** — 4 themed nodes along the pilgrimage: Five Elements Mountain (ore), Flower-Fruit Mountain (wood), Heavenly Court (star iron), Thunderclap Temple (sacred stones). Idle gathering up to 24h.

🏰 **Dungeons** — 4 new dungeons for rare forge materials. Dragon Palace (scales), Phoenix Terrace (feathers), Chaos Rift (essence), Hongmeng Trial (cores). Daily limited.

🐉 **Boss Mechanics** — 7 new combat mechanics: immunity, counter, multi-phase, enrage, shields, weakness, and one-shot skills. Brute force won't work anymore — you need the right weapon.

🔥 **Transmutation** — Combine 10 low-tier materials into 1 higher-tier.

Saves auto-migrate. Source: https://github.com/legendfz/idleGame 🙏
