**正文**:

v5.0 adds something idle games rarely have: meaningful build choices.

🌳 **Talent Trees** — 3 paths, ~25 total nodes:

```
⚔️ Combat   — ATK, crit rate, crit damage
🧘 Cultivate — cultivation speed, breakthrough rate
🔨 Craft    — forge success, gather speed
```

Talent points from realm breakthroughs (+1), prestige (+3), and special achievements (+1). On prestige, points carry over but talents reset — so you can respec every cycle.

This means each prestige run can have a different build: go full combat for boss rushing, full craft for gear farming, or hybrid.

🐾 **Companions** — 6 Journey-themed pets (Dragon Horse, White Dragon, Earth God, Mountain God, River God, Crane). Each gives a unique passive buff. Obtained from combat drops, dungeons, achievements. Upgradeable to level 10. Equip 1 at a time.

Your companion choice should match your talent build. Combat talent + White Dragon (ATK buff) = boss destroyer.
